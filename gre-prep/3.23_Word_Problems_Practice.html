Translation

Consecutive integer problem

Problem

The product of two consecutive integers is 72. What is the larger integer?

Solution

Step 1: Let the integers be $n$ and $n + 1$

Step 2: $n(n + 1) = 72$, so $n^2 + n - 72 = 0$

Step 3: $(n - 8)(n + 9) = 0$, so $n = 8$

Answer: $9$

Translation

Age problem

Problem

Kim is 8 years older than Lee. In 4 years, Kim will be twice Lee's current age. How old is Lee now?

Solution

Step 1: Kim $= L + 8$

Step 2: In 4 years: $(L + 8) + 4 = 2L$

Step 3: $L + 12 = 2L$, so $L = 12$

Answer: Lee is $12$ years old

Distance-Rate-Time

Catching up problem

Problem

Train A leaves at 9 AM at 50 mph. Train B leaves at 10 AM on the same route at 70 mph. When does B catch A?

Solution

Step 1: A's head start: $50 \times 1 = 50$ miles

Step 2: Closing speed: $70 - 50 = 20$ mph

Step 3: Time to close: $\frac{50}{20} = 2.5$ hours after 10 AM

Answer: 12:30 PM

Distance-Rate-Time

Round trip, different speeds

Problem

Sarah drove to work at 30 mph and returned at 50 mph. If the round trip took 4 hours, how far is her workplace?

Solution

Step 1: $\frac{d}{30} + \frac{d}{50} = 4$

Step 2: $\frac{5d}{150} + \frac{3d}{150} = 4$

Step 3: $\frac{8d}{150} = 4$, so $d = \frac{600}{8}$

Answer: $75$ miles

Work Rates

Pool with fill and drain

Problem

A pool fills in 8 hours and drains in 12 hours. With both open, how long to fill?

Solution

Step 1: Net rate: $\frac{1}{8} - \frac{1}{12}$

Step 2: $\frac{3}{24} - \frac{2}{24} = \frac{1}{24}$

Step 3: Flip the rate

Answer: $24$ hours

Interest

Compound interest

Problem

$2{,}000 compounded annually at 5% for 3 years yields what amount?

Solution

Formula: $A = P(1 + r)^t$

Plug in: $A = 2000(1.05)^3$

Calculate: $2000 \times 1.157625$

Answer: $\$2{,}315.25$

Overlapping Sets

Percentage-based sets

Problem

Every employee speaks English, Spanish, or both. If 80% speak English and 60% speak Spanish, what percent speak both?

Solution

Step 1: Neither $= 0$ (everyone speaks at least one)

Step 2: $100 = 80 + 60 - \text{Both}$

Step 3: $\text{Both} = 140 - 100$

Answer: $40\%$

Mixed Application

Mixture problem

Problem

A solution is 20% acid. How many liters of pure acid must be added to 10 liters to make it 50% acid?

Solution

Step 1: Acid now: $10 \times 0.20 = 2$ liters

Step 2: $\frac{2 + x}{10 + x} = 0.50$

Step 3: $2 + x = 5 + 0.5x$, so $0.5x = 3$

Answer: $6$ liters

Mixed Application

Split investment problem

Problem

Two investments totaling $10,000 earn 4% and 6%. If total interest is $520, how much is at 6%?

Solution

Step 1: Let $x$ = amount at 4%. Then $10000 - x$ at 6%.

Step 2: $0.04x + 0.06(10000 - x) = 520$

Step 3: $0.04x + 600 - 0.06x = 520$, so $x = 4000$

Answer: $\$6{,}000$ at 6%

Key Takeaways

Word problems practice

  • Translation: Assign variables, translate words to equations, solve algebraically.
  • Distance-Rate-Time: $d = rt$. For catch-up, use relative speed.
  • Work Rates: $\frac{1}{A} + \frac{1}{B} = \frac{1}{T}$. Subtract for drains.
  • Interest: Simple ($I = Prt$) vs. Compound ($A = P(1+r)^t$).
  • Overlapping Sets: Total $= A + B -$ Both $+$ Neither.
  • Mixtures: Track the quantity of interest (acid, money) through the change.

Session 3 Resource

Resume Gap Analysis Prompt

Use AI to compare your resume against any job posting. Get keyword match analysis, missing skills identification, and rewritten bullets.

The ATS 80/20 Rule

  • 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS before a human sees them
  • The top factors: job title match, skills keyword alignment, clean formatting
  • Focus on the 20% that matters most: title, hard skills, and quantified results
  • ATS doesn't penalize for extra content — it rewards for keyword matches

The Prompt

Copy this prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI tool. Paste your resume and target job posting where indicated.

I need you to perform a detailed gap analysis between my resume and a target job posting.

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Compare my resume against the job posting
2. Identify keyword matches and gaps
3. Suggest specific rewrites for my weakest bullets
4. Score my overall match

MY RESUME:
[PASTE YOUR FULL RESUME TEXT HERE]

TARGET JOB POSTING:
[PASTE THE FULL JOB POSTING HERE]

ANALYSIS REQUESTED:

1. KEYWORD MATCH ANALYSIS
   - List all keywords/skills from the job posting
   - Mark each as: ✅ Found in resume | ❌ Missing | ⚠️ Partially matched
   - Calculate overall match percentage

2. SKILLS GAP IDENTIFICATION
   - Hard skills missing from my resume that appear in the posting
   - Soft skills emphasized in the posting but absent from my resume
   - Industry-specific terminology I should incorporate

3. BULLET REWRITES (Top 5)
   - For the 5 resume bullets that are weakest relative to this specific job posting — the ones that miss the most critical requirements or keywords — provide a rewritten version that:
     a) Incorporates missing keywords naturally
     b) Starts with a strong action verb
     c) Includes a quantified result (ask me for numbers if needed)
     d) Answers "so what?" — why this accomplishment mattered

4. TITLE AND SUMMARY ALIGNMENT
   - Does my resume title/headline match the job posting's title?
   - Suggested headline rewrite if needed
   - 2-3 sentence summary optimized for this role

5. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
   - Match score: __/100
   - Top 3 strengths (what already aligns well)
   - Top 3 gaps (what to address first)
   - Honest verdict: Ready to apply / Needs moderate work / Significant gap

Works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI assistant.

Action Verbs by Category

Replace weak verbs like "responsible for" and "helped with." Start every bullet with one of these.

Leadership & Management

Directed, Spearheaded, Orchestrated, Championed, Oversaw, Mentored, Mobilized, Cultivated

Achievement & Results

Achieved, Delivered, Exceeded, Generated, Accelerated, Maximized, Surpassed, Captured

Analysis & Strategy

Analyzed, Assessed, Evaluated, Identified, Forecasted, Mapped, Diagnosed, Synthesized

Creation & Innovation

Designed, Developed, Built, Launched, Pioneered, Architected, Engineered, Established

Improvement & Optimization

Streamlined, Optimized, Transformed, Revamped, Elevated, Modernized, Consolidated, Restructured

Communication & Influence

Presented, Negotiated, Persuaded, Articulated, Influenced, Advocated, Facilitated, Brokered

Collaboration

Partnered, Coordinated, Aligned, Integrated, Unified, Collaborated

Tips for Using This Prompt

  • Run this prompt for each job you're seriously considering, not every posting you see
  • Focus on closing the top 3 gaps — you don't need 100% keyword match
  • Use the rewritten bullets as a starting point, then edit in your voice
  • Keep a "master resume" with all bullets, then customize for each application
  • Re-run the analysis after making changes to verify improvement

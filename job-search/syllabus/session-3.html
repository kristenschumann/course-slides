Session 3 of 6

Craft a Resume and LinkedIn to Impress Humans

Optimize your resume for ATS systems, rebuild your LinkedIn profile for recruiter visibility, and publish your first brand-building post. Three deliverables in 90 minutes.

ATS-optimized resume Refreshed LinkedIn profile Published LinkedIn post
90 min live session ~1 hr 15 min homework

Come Prepared With

  • Your #1 target job posting (full text, copied into a document)
  • Your current resume in an editable format (Google Doc or Word)
  • Your LinkedIn audit screenshots from Session 2 homework
  • 3 LinkedIn profiles you admire (bookmarked or screenshotted)
  • A laptop with LinkedIn open and your AI tool ready

Session Overview

Learning Objectives

  • Understand how ATS systems filter applications (and debunk the myths)
  • Use AI to perform a gap analysis between your resume and target job postings
  • Optimize LinkedIn headline, summary, and skills section for recruiter visibility
  • Develop a content strategy and publish your first brand-building LinkedIn post

90-Minute Agenda

5 min

Check-In

Networking wins from the week. Any responses to your outreach? Informational interviews scheduled? Celebrate the momentum.

10 min

ATS Demystified

How ATS systems parse resumes. What gets filtered and why. The 80/20: job title match, skills alignment, clean formatting. What recruiters scan in 10 seconds.

15 min

Live AI Demo: Resume Gap Analysis

Feed a real resume and job posting into AI. Get a detailed gap analysis: what matches, what's missing, and rewritten bullets. See the before/after live.

20 min

Hands-On: Optimize Your Resume

Run your own resume through the AI gap analysis against your target posting. Rewrite your top 3 bullets. Peer review in breakout rooms with the "so what?" test.

15 min

LinkedIn Profile Rebuild

AI-powered audit of your headline, about section, and skills. The formula: hook, story, proof, call to action. Update your profile live in the session.

15 min

Content Strategy: Draft and Publish

Three post formats that work: lesson learned, hot take, breakdown. Draft your first post with AI brainstorming. Hit publish before you leave.

10 min

Wrap-Up and Preview

Deliverable check: resume optimized, LinkedIn refreshed, post published. Homework assignments. Preview Session 4.

What You'll Build

ATS-Optimized Resume

Your resume rewritten with AI-identified gaps closed: matching job titles, aligned skills language, and impact-driven bullets that answer "so what?" for every line.

Homework

Here's what to do between Session 3 and Session 4. Focus on finishing what you started tonight.

This Week's Assignments

~1 hr 15 min

Finalize your ATS-optimized resume 30 min

Incorporate all feedback from the peer review. Run one final AI gap analysis to verify keyword coverage. Save as both an editable format and a PDF.

Complete your full LinkedIn profile update 25 min

Finish all sections: headline, about, experience (top 3 roles rewritten), skills (reordered for target role), and featured section. Update anything you didn't get to in session.

Publish a second LinkedIn post 15 min

Pick a different format from the one you used in session. The first post is the hardest. The second builds momentum. Try to post within 3-4 days of Session 3.

Brainstorm an AI project idea 20 min

Think about a workflow in your field that could be automated or improved with AI. Write a 2-3 sentence description of the problem and how AI could help. This is new and it's the foundation for Session 4.

How McKinsey Scores You

Product Score

  • Quality of your final answers
  • Did you get the right result?
  • Accuracy on each exercise

Process Score

  • How you arrived at answers
  • Clicking patterns and time allocation
  • Systematic vs. random approach
2:30 - 3:15 | Dual scoring changes how you approach this test. Rushing and guessing hurts your process score even if you land correct answers. Pass rate is 15-30% depending on office. Candidates who do 3-5 practice runs perform measurably better than those going in cold.

Pymetrics: 12 Mini-Games

Balloons

Pump for money, risk popping. Measures risk tolerance.

Towers

Rearrange disks on pegs. Measures planning ability.

Digits

Recall number sequences. Measures working memory.

Plus 9 more games testing attention, emotion, fairness, focus, generosity, and learning. 25-35 minutes total. Your results are compared to the trait profile of successful BCG employees.

3:20 - 4:15 | 12 mini-games measuring 91 cognitive traits across 9 categories. Pymetrics is the one format where extensive preparation has diminishing returns. The games capture natural cognitive responses. Best strategy: take the free practice round, then test well-rested and calm.

Casey: Online Case Assessment

  • 8-10 questions in 25-30 minutes, calculators allowed
  • Mixed format: multiple-choice, numerical fill-in, and written responses
  • 1-minute video recording of your final recommendation
  • One attempt only as of 2026 (previously two attempts)
4:20 - 5:30 | Casey tests structuring, case math, chart interpretation, and synthesis. Calculators allowed means more complex multi-step calculations. One attempt rule makes preparation essential. Treat the 1-minute video as a mini-presentation, not an afterthought.

Video Interview Essentials

Format

  • 3-8 behavioral questions on screen
  • 30 sec prep, 1-3 min to record each
  • BCG uses SparkHire (~45 min total)

What Gets Evaluated

  • Verbal content and STAR structure
  • Pacing, clarity, keyword specificity
  • Facial analysis phased out in 2021
5:35 - 6:30 | No interviewer on the other end. AI does NOT score facial expressions. STAR method is essential. Prepare 8-10 stories covering leadership, teamwork, problem-solving, failure, and motivation. Record yourself at least 5 days before the real interview.

Key Takeaways

What to Remember

  • Digital assessments are the biggest filter. 70-85% of candidates past resume screening get eliminated here.
  • McKinsey Solve tests answers and process. 3-5 practice simulation runs are the highest-ROI prep investment.
  • Pymetrics measures natural traits. You cannot game it. Take the free practice, then test well-rested.
  • Casey is a timed online case. Your module prep transfers directly, but respect the one-attempt rule.
  • Record yourself on camera. Almost nobody practices video interviews, so basic prep gives a huge edge.

Session Overview

Learning Objectives

  • Identify the right people to contact at target companies (peers, hiring managers, connectors)

90-Minute Agenda

5 min

Check-In

How did your AI agent perform this week? Quick wins and surprises from daily matching. Celebrate what your agent surfaced.

10 min

The Networking Mindset Shift

Why networking isn't "using people." Up to 70% of roles are filled through connections before they're posted. The reframe: you're learning from people, not asking for favors.

15 min

Live AI Demo: Personalized Outreach

Side-by-side comparison of AI-only vs. AI-assisted outreach. Watch how adding your research notes transforms a generic message into one that gets replies.

25 min

Hands-On: Craft and Send Your Messages

Write 5 personalized outreach messages using the AI-assisted method. Peer review in breakout rooms. Then hit send. Not drafts. Sent.

20 min

Informational Interview Masterclass + Role-Play

The 5-question framework that leads to referrals. Live role-play practice in pairs. Learn how one conversation turns into three by always asking "who else should I talk to?"

15 min

Wrap-Up and Preview

Deliverable check: 5 messages sent, role-play completed. Homework assignments. Preview Session 3.

Homework

Here's what to do between Session 2 and Session 3. The outreach messages are the priority.

This Week's Assignments

~1.5 hrs over the week

Send 5 more outreach messages 20 min

Expand your outreach to 10 total. Track who responds and who doesn't. Note patterns in what works. You proved tonight that you can do it. Now build the habit.

Conduct 1-2 informational interviews 1 hr

If any connections respond positively, schedule and complete at least one informational interview using the 5-question framework. Send a thank-you within 24 hours that references something specific they said.

Audit your LinkedIn profile 10 min

Screenshot your current LinkedIn headline, summary, and experience section. Note what feels outdated or generic. Bring these screenshots to Session 3.

Keep running your AI agent daily 10 min/day

Don't let the daily habit slip. Your target list should be growing and sharpening each day.

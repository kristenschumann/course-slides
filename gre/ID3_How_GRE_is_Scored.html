Do Well on Section 1

You get a harder Section 2. Questions in the harder section are worth more toward your final score.

This is where high scores come from.

Struggle on Section 1

You get an easier Section 2. Even getting most questions right won't yield as high a score.

The scoring ceiling is lower.

Key strategy: Don't rush through Section 1. If running low on time, guess rather than leaving questions blank.

3:30 - 5:00 | Explain section-adaptive scoring. First section performance determines second section difficulty. Doing well unlocks harder questions worth more points. Key strategy: don't rush Section 1, always guess rather than skip.

Program Expectations

What Scores Mean for Admissions

Different programs weight sections differently. There's no universal "good" GRE score. What matters is how your score compares to your target programs.

STEM Programs

Typically prioritize Quant scores. A 165 Quant with 155 Verbal might be perfectly fine.

Focus: Quantitative

Humanities & Social Sciences

Often prioritize Verbal scores. A 165 Verbal with 155 Quant might work well.

Focus: Verbal

MBA & Business

Typically want strong scores in both. Top programs often look for 160+ in each section.

Focus: Both sections

Research your target programs. Find their median GRE scores and aim to match or exceed them.

5:00 - 6:30 | Different programs care about different sections. STEM focuses on Quant. Humanities on Verbal. MBA wants both. There's no universal good score. Research your specific programs.

Reporting Options

Score Choice & Reporting

01

ScoreSelect

Choose which test date's scores to send. If you take the GRE multiple times, you can send only your best scores to programs.

02

Superscoring

Some schools take your highest Verbal from one date and highest Quant from another. Check with your programs to see if they superscore.

03

5-Year Validity

Scores are valid for 5 years from your test date. You have flexibility in when you apply to programs.

About canceling scores: You can cancel immediately after the test before seeing them. But most students don't need to worry about this. Take the test, see your scores, and if needed, retake and use ScoreSelect.

6:30 - 7:30 | Cover score reporting options. ScoreSelect lets you choose which dates to send. Some schools superscore. Scores valid 5 years. Most students don't need to worry about canceling.

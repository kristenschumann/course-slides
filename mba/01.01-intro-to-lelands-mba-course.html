Course Overview

What This Course Covers

  • Strategy and planning: decide if an MBA is right for you, build your school list
  • Testing: choose between GMAT and GRE, balance prep with applications
  • Your story: craft your narrative, position your background, set career goals
  • Written materials: resume, essays, activities, and polishing
  • Recommendations and interviews: choose recommenders, prepare for interviews
  • Final steps: post-submission strategy, waitlists, and preparation
0:30 - 1:30 | This course covers every major component of the MBA application. We start with strategy: is an MBA right for you, what type of program, building your school list. Then testing, choosing GMAT vs GRE. Your narrative and career goals. All your written materials from resume through essays. Recommendations and interviews. And finally what happens after you submit. You will also get templates, exercises, and examples from real admits.

Session 1 of 6

Build Your AI Job-Search Engine

Build an AI agent that searches the internet to find realistic, high-potential opportunities for you. By the end of tonight, you'll have a working tool you can use tomorrow morning.

Deliverable: A working AI agent that sources opportunities daily
90 min live session ~1.5 hrs homework

Come Prepared With

  • Your career criteria notes (function, culture, growth, comp)
  • 5-10 saved job postings in a document you can copy-paste from
  • A laptop with your AI tool (Claude or ChatGPT) logged in and ready
  • Something to take notes (digital or paper)

Session Overview

Learning Objectives

  • Define personal role-fit criteria across function, culture, growth, and comp dimensions
  • Use AI to batch-analyze job postings and identify high-match opportunities
  • Build a reusable AI agent/prompt for daily job matching
  • Create a curated shortlist of 10 target roles with rationale

90-Minute Agenda

5 min

Welcome and Introductions

Meet your cohort. Quick intros: your name, target role, and how long you've been searching.

10 min

The Strategy Problem

Why spray-and-pray fails. 59% of job seekers search 3+ months. The shift from "fix my resume" to "fix my strategy."

15 min

The Role-Fit Scorecard

Four dimensions of fit: function, culture, growth, compensation. You'll fill in your own scorecard with must-haves, nice-to-haves, and dealbreakers.

20 min

Live AI Demo: Job Matching

Watch AI analyze 50+ postings against a Role-Fit Scorecard in real time. See how it ranks, scores, and explains its reasoning.

25 min

Hands-On Workshop

Build your own AI matching prompt. Test it with your pre-work job postings. Refine criteria in real time. Peer feedback in breakout rooms.

10 min

Daily Automation Setup

Three ways to automate your search: saved prompt, custom GPT, or full automation. Set up your daily 10-minute routine before you leave.

5 min

Wrap-Up and Preview

Review what you built. Homework assignments. Preview Session 2.

Homework

Here's what to do between Session 1 and Session 2. The daily agent is the most important one.

This Week's Assignments

~1.5 hrs over the week

Run your AI agent daily 10 min/day

Every morning, paste fresh job postings into your prompt and review the top matches. This is the habit that compounds. 7 days x 10 minutes = 70 matched roles by next session.

Refine your Role-Fit Scorecard 15 min

After a few days of running your agent, adjust your criteria. Too many matches? Tighten your requirements. Too few? Broaden one dimension. Your scorecard should feel sharp by Session 2.

Finalize your top 10 target roles 20 min

Lock in your shortlist of 10 roles from your agent's output. For each, note why it scored high on your scorecard.

Identify 5 people at target companies 15 min

For your top 3-5 companies, find one person at each you'd like to connect with. Look for peers in similar roles, hiring managers, or alumni from your school. We'll use this list in Session 2.

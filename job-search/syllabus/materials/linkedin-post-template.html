Tips for LinkedIn Posts About AI Projects

  • The hook is everything. Write 5 versions and pick the most specific one. Generic hooks like "AI is changing everything" get scrolled past instantly.
  • Lead with the problem, not the technology. "I automated a 4-hour task" beats "I built a Python script using the OpenAI API." People care about outcomes, not tools.
  • Include a number. Posts with quantified results get 2-3x more engagement. Time saved, accuracy improved, hours reclaimed â€” pick the most impressive metric.
  • End with a genuine question, not a generic "thoughts?" Ask something people have an opinion about. "What repetitive task would you automate first?" invites real responses.
  • Post Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM. Engage with every comment for the first hour. The algorithm rewards early engagement, and your replies keep the post visible longer.

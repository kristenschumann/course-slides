Job Search Bootcamp

Four Dimensions of Fit

Function

Function Fit

Day-to-day work matches your skills and interests

Culture

Culture Fit

Values, pace, and working style align with yours

Growth

Growth Trajectory

Clear path to where you want to be in 2-3 years

Comp

Compensation

Salary, equity, and benefits meet your floor

13:15 - 15:00 | Introduce the four dimensions. One sentence each. COACH: "These are the four dimensions. Every role gets scored on all four. No single dimension is enough on its own." Key point: Fit is multi-dimensional. A great salary with bad culture is still a bad fit.

Scoring Your Criteria

Dimension Must-Have Nice-to-Have Dealbreaker

Culture Remote-friendly Flat hierarchy Always-on Slack culture
Growth Path to director in 3 yrs Mentorship program No internal mobility
Comp $150K+ base Equity upside Below $130K

Prompt Structure

Part 1: "Here are my role-fit criteria: [paste scorecard]"

Job Search Bootcamp

Prompt Engineering Tips

Do
  • Be specific about your criteria
  • Include dealbreakers explicitly
  • Ask for reasoning, not just scores
Avoid
  • Vague instructions ("find good jobs")
  • Skipping the scoring rubric
  • Trusting AI scores without reading the posting
40:00 - 42:00 | Quick prompt engineering tips. Keep it practical. COACH: "Three dos and three don'ts. The biggest mistake is being vague. 'Find me good jobs' gets you garbage. 'Score these against my four criteria and explain each score' gets you gold."

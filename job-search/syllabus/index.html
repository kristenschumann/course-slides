After the Bootcamp

Your 30-day launch plan

The bootcamp gives you the system. The 30 days after are where you put it to work. Here's what your routine looks like.

  • Run your AI agent every morning (10 minutes). Review top matches and add the best ones to your target list.
  • Post on LinkedIn once per week. Share insights, lessons, or updates from your search. Consistency builds visibility.
  • Send 5-10 personalized outreach messages per week. Use the templates from Session 2. Focus on quality over quantity.
  • Practice one mock interview per week. Use AI or a peer pod partner. Reps build confidence.
  • Track your metrics. Applications sent, callbacks received, interviews landed, offers made. What gets measured gets managed.
  • Stay active in the alumni Slack. Share wins, ask for advice, and hold each other accountable.

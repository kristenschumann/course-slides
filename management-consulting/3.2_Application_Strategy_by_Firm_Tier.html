Break Into Management Consulting

Three-Tier Application Portfolio

Reach (20-30%)

MBB and top Tier 2 firms where you are competitive but not guaranteed. Target 3 firms.

Target (40-50%)

Tier 2 firms where your profile aligns well with their needs. Target 4-6 firms.

Safety (20-30%)

Tier 2 and boutique firms where you exceed the typical profile. Target 3-5 firms.

0:55 - 2:00 | Walk through each tier. Reach firms are MBB plus ultra-selective Tier 2 (20-30% of list). Target firms are Tier 2 where your background genuinely aligns (40-50%, bulk of effort). Safety firms are smaller Tier 2 and boutiques where you would be strong (20-30%). Total: 10-20 firms. This is strategy, not desperation.

Most candidates sequence backward: they apply to MBB first out of excitement, then scramble to add Tier 2.

Mistakes That Kill Strategies

  • Applying only to MBB. With 1% acceptance rates, you need more than 3-5 shots.
  • Not customizing materials. Tier 2 firms can tell when they are your backup plan.
  • Missing rolling deadlines. Tier 2 firms fill spots throughout the year.
  • Ignoring firm specializations. Applying to LEK without PE interest hurts credibility.
6:00 - 6:30 | Every mistake is preventable. The portfolio approach solves MBB-only applications. Genuine research (covered in resource 3.3) solves generic materials. Disciplined timeline management prevents missed deadlines. The Tier 2 strengths table solves the specialization mismatch problem.

Key Takeaways

Application Strategy by Firm Tier

  • Build a portfolio of 10-20 firms across three tiers: reach (MBB, 20-30%), target (Tier 2 aligned, 40-50%), and safety (boutique, 20-30%).
  • Tier 2 firms are strategic choices. LEK for PE, Oliver Wyman for financial services, Kearney for operations. Match your interests to firm strengths.
  • Sequence applications practice-first. Submit safety and boutique apps early, Tier 2 next, MBB last so you interview sharp.
  • Track everything. Use the Application Tracker in resource 3.4 to manage deadlines, interviews, and follow-ups across all firms.
6:30 - 7:00 | Four takeaways. One: build a balanced portfolio of 10-20 firms across three tiers. Two: Tier 2 firms are strategic choices, not consolation prizes. Three: sequence applications so you practice at safety firms before performing at MBB. Four: track everything with the application tracker template.

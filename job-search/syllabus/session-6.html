Ace Cases, Take-Homes, and Live Challenges

Master the final hurdle between you and the offer: skills assessments. Then assemble your complete job search playbook and launch your 30-day execution plan.

Deliverable: Practice assessment + complete playbook + 30-day plan
90 min live session ~30 min pre-work 30-day plan replaces homework

Pre-Work

Complete these before Session 6. This is the last time you'll prepare. Make it count.

Before You Arrive

~30 min total

1 Review your practice assessment example 10 min

Read through the sample case study, take-home, or presentation prompt provided in the session materials. Don't try to solve it yet. Just understand the format and what's being asked.

2 Reflect on your bootcamp journey 10 min

Review your deliverables from Sessions 1-5: your AI agent, outreach templates, resume, LinkedIn profile, AI project, and STAR stories. What's working? What still needs attention?

3 Write your biggest remaining concern 10 min

One paragraph, as specific as possible. Not "I'm nervous about interviews" but "I freeze when asked follow-up questions during behavioral rounds." This helps your coach address real gaps during the final session.

What to Bring

  • A practice assessment example relevant to your target role (case study, take-home prompt, or presentation brief)
  • Your complete interview prep materials from Sessions 1-5
  • Your STAR answer bank (aim for 8-10 answers)
  • Laptop with Google Docs or slides ready for the timed practice assessment
  • Your one-paragraph concern for the group Q&A

Session Overview

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the different assessment types and what each one is really evaluating
  • Apply a universal framework for approaching any skills assessment with confidence
  • Use AI strategically during assessments (when it helps vs. when it hurts)
  • Complete a timed practice assessment and get structured peer feedback

90-Minute Agenda

5 min

Check-In and Celebration

Final session kickoff. Mock interview wins, real interview updates, celebrate how far everyone has come.

15 min

The Assessment Landscape

Four assessment types (take-home, case, live challenge, presentation), what each one tests on the surface vs. what it really evaluates, and how to use AI strategically.

10 min

The 5-Step Framework

Clarify, Structure, Execute, Polish, Present. One approach that works for every assessment type. Deep dive on each step with examples.

25 min

Timed Practice Assessment

Choose your track: case study, take-home prompt, or presentation outline. 25 minutes under real conditions with a visible timer.

15 min

Peer Review

Present your work in groups of 3-4. Structured feedback on Clarify, Structure, Execute, Polish, and Present using a rubric.

10 min

Playbook Assembly

Pull all 6 sessions together. Link every deliverable into one document. Add your weekly action checklist.

10 min

Celebration and Launch

What you built in 6 weeks. Your 30-day challenge. Metrics to track. Alumni community. Final commitments.

Your 30-Day Execution Plan

This is the final session, so there's no homework. Instead, here's the system you'll run for the next 30 days. This is how you turn everything you've built into offers.

Daily

Every Morning

Run your AI job search agent. Review top matches. Apply to the strongest 1-2 roles. Total: 10-15 minutes.

Weekly

Every Week

Post on LinkedIn once. Send 5-10 personalized outreach messages. Apply to 3-5 high-fit roles. Practice one mock interview.

Ongoing

Continuous

Prep for assessments using the 5-step framework. Refine your STAR stories after each interview. Update your playbook as you learn what works.

Track Your Progress

Metric Target How to Track
Applications sent 15-20 per month Weekly count in your playbook
Callbacks / responses 20-30% response rate Note which outreach approach worked
Interviews completed 4-6 per month Debrief after each one
Offers 1+ in 60 days Celebrate every single one
What gets measured gets managed. Track these four numbers weekly. When your callback rate drops, revisit your outreach. When interviews aren't converting, revisit your STAR stories. The data tells you where to focus.
